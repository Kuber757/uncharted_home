A Quest for Perfection

Our Story

We roam the earth to source a vast array of unique materials and seek to harness untapped skills of master craftsmen to curate bespoke designs of unmatched quality. We collaborate closely with and inspire designers with the exquisite materials we discover. At Uncharted, we are navigators who believe creativity is a spontaneous journey and approach all our projects with no predetermined notions of a destination. We create truly unique and inimitable products that result from a combination of one-of-a-kind materials, stunning craftsmanship and delightful design.

A Quest for Perfection

おもてなし

We strive to implement a holistic approach in each of our projects to create a sense of uniformity. At Uncharted we draw parallels with the Japanese philosophy of omotenashi which emphasizes exceptional service to the customer. The customer and their individual desires and design expectations form the focus of each of our projects. We are committed to not only understanding our customers’ needs and desires, but also go the extra mile to anticipate and exceed their expectations through our design aesthetic that is truly extraordinary. Our mesmerising designs are crafted with utmost attention-to-detail, creating a harmonious balance of style and purpose.
