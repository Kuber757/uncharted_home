ものづくり

The Spirit of Monozukuri

When it comes to selecting craftsmen and manufacturers, we work to Japanese monozukuri principles. It underlines that manufacturing should be in harmony with nature and should be of value to the society. When an item or human effort is used, there needs to be a benefit for the society and, at the same time, the balance between production, resources and the society should be maintained.
